We believe that beauty thrives in diversity and discovery. Our purpose is to expand the way the world sees beauty by empowering the extraordinary in each one of us. Orchid Lux Cosmetics believes in a world that champions all beauty. A world where each one of us feels celebrated for who we are, not what others would like us to be. From the very beginning, our founder believed that Orchid Lux Cosmetics could not only disrupt beauty retail, but that it would have a positive impact on the world.

At Orchid Lux Cosmetics, we offer the latest, most innovative and unique beauty selection. Serving our customers has been our ultimate goal since the beginning. Our joy comes from delivering a curated, innovative prestige beauty experience, giving people the freedom to explore their beauty and meet the best version of themselves. Having trust in beauty retailers should not be considered a luxury. Our ultimate goal is to sustainably bring luxury experience to every single one of our customers. The trust our customers put in our brand has proven to be the biggest capital company has acquired over the course of our many years of operation. We believe in your beauty, so we ask you to have faith in our sustainable business.

We love to discover the best brands and products across the world, so we can offer our customers the most efficient formulas, inclusive ranges, and environmentally responsible selections. We have been disrupting the norms of the beauty industry since the beginning, allowing customers to freely explore and try beauty products for the first time.

Since then, we continuously set new standards to reimagine the Orchid Lux Cosmetics beauty journey and provide a seamless, personalized and inspiring experience at each step, across all our channels. We work with our partners to deliver the best in beauty every day. We place the customer at the heart of every decision we make, from product assortment, to in store experiences.

Our Beauty advisors are trained across multiple brands and product categories to build personalized recommendations and create unforgettable experiences for our customers. Our years long experience has proven to be an incredibly successful means of establishing a genuine connection with both our suppliers of sustainable goods, as well as our customers.

We have nothing to hide, and we proudly disclose every hand-selected ingredient used in our products, including fragrances (which are never synthetic). You should feel free to trust your beauty products, so ours are free of: parabens, gluten, synthetic fragrances, GMOs, sulfate, pthalates, talc. And we would never test on animals.

First and foremost, we are highly inclined to believe that it is of utmost importance to prioritise sustainable and environmentally friendly ingredients. This means searching for ingredients that are ethically grown, harvested, manufactured and distributed with a minimal impact on the planet. We asure you our ingredients are certified organic, fair trade and sustainably harvested. Using local and organic ingredients is another important step towards sustainability. This can not only help reduce carbon emissions associated with transportation, but it also supports local businesses and allows greater oversight of the ingredients used in our products. That is why we pride ourselves in sourcing most of our ingredients are locally.
